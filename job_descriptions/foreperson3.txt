Job Title: Construction Crew Foreperson

Company: MNO General Subcontractor

Description:
MNO General Subcontractor is seeking a reliable and experienced Construction Crew Foreperson to lead and manage our construction teams. The successful candidate will be responsible for ensuring the timely and efficient completion of construction projects while maintaining safety and quality standards.

Responsibilities:

Oversee day-to-day operations of construction crews, including assigning tasks, setting goals, and providing guidance.
Review and interpret project plans, specifications, and schedules to ensure work is completed accurately and on time.
Ensure adherence to safety protocols, company policies, and industry standards.
Communicate with project managers, subcontractors, and other project stakeholders to ensure smooth project execution.
Monitor and evaluate crew performance, providing feedback and coaching as needed.
Maintain accurate records of crew activities, materials, and equipment usage.
Troubleshoot and resolve any issues or conflicts that may arise on-site, coordinating with project managers and other stakeholders as necessary.
Requirements:

High school diploma or equivalent; additional education in construction management, engineering, or a related field is a plus.
A minimum of 5 years of experience in the construction industry, with at least 3 years in a supervisory or leadership role.
Comprehensive knowledge of construction methods, materials, tools, and safety protocols.
Ability to read and interpret construction drawings, specifications, and contracts.
Strong leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.
Able to work well under pressure and adapt to changing priorities and deadlines.
Valid driver's license and willingness to travel to various project sites as needed.
Optional Requirements:

Certification or training in construction safety, such as OSHA 30-hour certification.
Experience in specialized construction sectors, such as commercial, residential, or infrastructure projects.
Bilingual or multilingual abilities, particularly in languages relevant to the local construction workforce.
