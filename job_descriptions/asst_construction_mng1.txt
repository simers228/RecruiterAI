Job Title: Assistant Project Manager (Construction)

Company: DEF General Subcontractor

Description:
DEF General Subcontractor is looking for a dedicated and organized Assistant Project Manager to join our team. The successful candidate will support our Project Managers in the successful execution of construction projects. This role offers excellent opportunities for growth and development within the company.

Responsibilities:

Assist Project Managers in the planning, execution, and closeout of construction projects.
Coordinate with clients, architects, engineers, subcontractors, and other project stakeholders.
Review project plans, specifications, and contract documents to ensure accuracy and completeness.
Help manage project resources, including personnel, equipment, and materials.
Monitor project progress and maintain project documentation, including schedules, budgets, and progress reports.
Assist in the identification and resolution of project issues, risks, and conflicts.
Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations, as well as industry standards and best practices.
Attend project meetings and site visits as required.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or a related field.
At least 2 years of experience in construction or project management, preferably with a general subcontractor.
Strong understanding of construction processes, materials, and quality standards.
Proficient in project management software, such as Procore, Buildertrend, or MS Project.
Excellent communication, organizational, and problem-solving skills.
Ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.
Valid driver's license and willingness to travel to project sites as needed.
Optional Requirements:

Certification or coursework in project management, such as CAPM (Certified Associate in Project Management) or other relevant credentials.
Experience with specific construction sectors, such as residential, commercial, or infrastructure projects.
Knowledge of relevant construction software, such as AutoCAD, Revit, or Bluebeam.
