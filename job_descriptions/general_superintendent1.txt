Job Title: General Superintendent

Company: VWX General Subcontractor

Description:
VWX General Subcontractor is seeking a highly skilled and experienced General Superintendent to oversee the successful completion of our construction projects. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in managing construction teams, ensuring work quality, and adhering to project schedules.

Responsibilities:

Oversee and manage all on-site construction activities, ensuring projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.
Supervise and coordinate construction crews, subcontractors, and other on-site personnel.
Develop and implement project schedules, making adjustments as necessary to meet deadlines and address unforeseen challenges.
Ensure all work is performed in compliance with safety regulations, company policies, and industry best practices.
Collaborate with project managers, clients, architects, and engineers to address project-related issues and concerns.
Regularly inspect work in progress to identify potential quality or safety issues and take corrective action as needed.
Maintain accurate records of project activities, including daily reports, safety logs, and progress updates.
Conduct project closeout activities, such as punch lists, final inspections, and warranty documentation.

Requirements:

High school diploma or equivalent; additional education in construction management or a related field is a plus.
Minimum of 10 years of experience in the construction industry, with at least 5 years in a supervisory or leadership role.
In-depth knowledge of construction methods, materials, tools, and safety protocols.
Ability to read and interpret construction drawings, specifications, and contracts.
Strong leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.
Willingness to work extended hours and weekends as needed to meet project deadlines.
Valid driver's license and ability to travel to various project sites.
