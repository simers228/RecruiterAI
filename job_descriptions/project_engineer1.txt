Job Title: Project Engineer

Company: XYZ General Subcontractor

Description:
XYZ General Subcontractor is seeking a highly motivated and organized Project Engineer to support our construction projects. The successful candidate will be responsible for assisting project managers with planning, scheduling, and coordinating project activities.

Responsibilities:

Support project managers with planning and scheduling project activities, such as procurement, construction, testing, and commissioning.
Review and interpret construction drawings and specifications to ensure compliance with project requirements.
Collaborate with project managers, superintendents, and subcontractors to ensure project timelines and budgets are met.
Monitor construction progress and identify potential issues or delays, providing recommendations for corrective action as needed.
Assist in managing project budgets and maintaining accurate project documentation.
Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations, company policies, and industry standards.
Participate in project meetings, providing technical expertise and support as needed.
Perform other duties as assigned by the project manager.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering, Construction Management, or a related field.
At least 3 years of experience in construction project engineering.
Knowledge of construction processes, materials, and safety regulations.
Strong communication, organization, and problem-solving skills.
Proficiency in project management software and tools.
Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
Valid driver's license and ability to travel to project sites as needed.
