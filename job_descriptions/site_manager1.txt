Job Title: Site Manager

Company: XYZ General Subcontractor

Description:
XYZ General Subcontractor is seeking a highly skilled and motivated Site Manager to oversee our construction projects. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing daily on-site operations, ensuring projects are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Responsibilities:

Oversee daily on-site construction activities, ensuring work is completed according to project specifications and schedules.
Coordinate construction crews, subcontractors, and other on-site personnel, providing guidance and direction as needed.
Monitor project schedules and budgets, making adjustments as needed to meet changing conditions and deadlines.
Collaborate with project managers, clients, architects, and other project stakeholders to address project-related issues and ensure smooth project execution.
Enforce compliance with health and safety regulations, company policies, and industry standards.
Inspect work in progress to maintain quality control and identify potential issues or areas for improvement.
Maintain accurate and up-to-date project documentation, such as daily logs, progress reports, and safety records.

Requirements:

High school diploma or equivalent; additional education in construction management or a related field is a plus.
At least 5 years of experience in the construction industry, with a focus on site management.
Knowledge of construction methods, materials, tools, and safety protocols.
Ability to read and interpret construction drawings, specifications, and contracts.
Strong communication, organizational, and problem-solving skills.
Willingness to work extended hours and weekends as needed to meet project deadlines.
Valid driver's license and ability to travel to various project sites.
