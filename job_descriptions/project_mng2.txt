Job Title: Construction Project Manager

Company: ABC General Subcontractor

Description:
ABC General Subcontractor is looking for a skilled Construction Project Manager to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing multiple construction projects from inception to completion, ensuring projects are delivered on time and within budget.

Responsibilities:

Develop project objectives and establish project plans, including scope, budget, and schedule.
Coordinate with clients, architects, engineers, and other project stakeholders.
Oversee subcontractor and supplier selection, contract negotiation, and contract management.
Conduct site visits to monitor progress and ensure work is performed in compliance with project specifications.
Resolve any issues or conflicts that may arise during the project lifecycle.
Ensure compliance with health, safety, and environmental regulations.
Maintain detailed project documentation and records.
Evaluate project outcomes and provide recommendations for improvement.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or a related field.
Minimum of 5 years of experience in construction project management, with a focus on subcontractor management.
Strong understanding of construction methodologies, materials, and best practices.
Proficient in MS Project or other project management software.
Excellent communication, organizational, and leadership skills.
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and adapt to changing priorities.
Valid driver's license and willingness to travel to project sites.
Optional Requirements:

Certified Construction Manager (CCM) or Project Management Professional (PMP) certification.
Experience managing projects in a specific niche, such as healthcare, retail, or education.
Bilingual or multilingual abilities, particularly in languages relevant to the local construction workforce.
