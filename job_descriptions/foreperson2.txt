Job Title: Site Foreperson

Company: JKL General Subcontractor

Description:
JKL General Subcontractor is looking for a skilled Site Foreperson to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing daily operations at our construction sites, ensuring work is completed efficiently and according to project specifications.

Responsibilities:

Manage and supervise construction crews, including hiring, training, and performance evaluation.
Review project plans, schedules, and budgets to ensure work is completed on time and within scope.
Ensure all work is performed in accordance with safety regulations, company policies, and industry standards.
Coordinate with project managers, subcontractors, and suppliers to ensure smooth project execution.
Monitor progress and quality of work, providing guidance and direction as needed.
Maintain accurate records of crew activities, materials, and equipment usage.
Resolve any issues or conflicts that may arise on-site, escalating to project managers as necessary.
Requirements:

High school diploma or equivalent, with further education in construction management, engineering, or a related field preferred.
Minimum of 5 years of experience in the construction industry, including at least 3 years in a supervisory or leadership role.
In-depth knowledge of construction processes, materials, and safety regulations.
Ability to read and interpret construction drawings, specifications, and contracts.
Strong leadership, communication, and conflict resolution skills.
Valid driver's license and ability to travel to various project sites.
