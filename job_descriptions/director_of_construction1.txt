Job Title: Director of Construction

Company: PQR General Subcontractor

Description:
PQR General Subcontractor is seeking a highly experienced and results-driven Director of Construction to oversee our construction operations. The successful candidate will be responsible for managing all aspects of construction projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Responsibilities:

Develop and implement strategic plans to ensure the successful execution of construction projects.
Oversee the management of multiple construction projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.
Lead and mentor project managers, superintendents, and other construction personnel, providing guidance and support as needed.
Foster and maintain strong relationships with clients, architects, engineers, and other project stakeholders.
Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations, company policies, and industry best practices.
Monitor project progress, budgets, and schedules, making adjustments as necessary to address unforeseen challenges or opportunities.
Collaborate with preconstruction and estimating teams to develop accurate project scopes, budgets, and schedules.
Stay current on industry trends, best practices, and market conditions to maintain a competitive edge.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or a related field.
A minimum of 15 years of experience in the construction industry, with at least 8 years in a leadership role.
Proven track record of successfully managing complex construction projects.
Strong knowledge of construction methods, materials, tools, and safety protocols.
Excellent leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills.
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and adapt to changing priorities and deadlines.
Valid driver's license and willingness to travel as needed.
