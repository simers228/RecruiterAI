Job Title: Director of Construction Operations

Company: UVW General Subcontractor

Description:
UVW General Subcontractor is seeking an experienced and dynamic Director of Construction Operations to lead our construction management team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing all aspects of construction projects, ensuring they are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Responsibilities:

Oversee the planning, execution, and completion of multiple construction projects, ensuring they meet quality, cost, and schedule objectives.
Provide leadership and guidance to project managers, superintendents, and other construction personnel.
Develop and implement construction strategies, processes, and best practices to drive efficiency and continuous improvement.
Foster and maintain strong relationships with clients, subcontractors, and other project stakeholders.
Ensure compliance with health and safety regulations, company policies, and industry standards.
Collaborate with preconstruction and estimating teams to develop accurate project scopes, budgets, and schedules.
Regularly review project progress, budgets, and schedules, taking corrective action as needed.
Stay current on industry trends, best practices, and market conditions to maintain a competitive edge.
Participate in the hiring, training, and development of construction personnel.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Civil Engineering, or a related field.
At least 15 years of experience in the construction industry, with a minimum of 8 years in a leadership role.
Demonstrated success in managing complex construction projects from start to finish.
In-depth knowledge of construction processes, materials, and safety regulations.
Strong leadership, communication, and negotiation skills.
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and adapt to changing priorities and deadlines.
Valid driver's license and ability to travel to project sites as required.
Optional Requirements:

Advanced degree in Construction Management, Business Administration, or a related field.
Professional certifications, such as the Project Management Professional (PMP) or the Associate Constructor (AC).
Experience in specific construction sectors, such as residential, commercial, or infrastructure projects.
