Job Title: Safety and Environmental Engineer

Company: ABC General Subcontractor

Description:
ABC General Subcontractor is seeking a skilled and experienced Safety and Environmental Engineer to ensure the safety of our employees, subcontractors, and the environment. The successful candidate will be responsible for developing and implementing safety and environmental programs and procedures that comply with applicable regulations and standards.

Responsibilities:

Develop, implement, and monitor safety and environmental programs, policies, and procedures to ensure compliance with safety and environmental regulations and standards.
Conduct safety and environmental inspections and audits of job sites, identifying potential hazards and risks and taking corrective action as needed.
Provide safety and environmental training to employees and subcontractors on safety and environmental procedures and practices.
Conduct environmental risk assessments and ensure compliance with environmental regulations and requirements.
Investigate accidents, incidents, and near-misses, identifying root causes and developing corrective action plans to prevent future occurrences.
Maintain safety and environmental records and documentation, such as safety and environmental plans, data sheets, and training records.
Develop and implement emergency response plans and procedures for safety and environmental incidents.
Collaborate with project managers, superintendents, and other construction personnel to ensure safety and environmental considerations are incorporated into project planning and execution.

Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Environmental Engineering, Occupational Health and Safety, or a related field.
At least 5 years of experience in construction safety and environmental management, with a strong knowledge of safety and environmental regulations, standards, and best practices.
Ability to perform safety and environmental inspections, identify hazards and risks, and develop and implement corrective actions.
Strong communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills.
Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
Proficiency in safety and environmental management software and databases.
Valid driver's license and ability to travel to job sites as needed.

Optional Requirements:

Professional certification in safety and environmental management, such as the Certified Safety Professional (CSP), the Certified Environmental Professional (CEP), or the Environmental Health and Safety Professional (EHS).
