Job Title: Chief Estimator

Company: GHI General Subcontractor

Description:
GHI General Subcontractor is seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Chief Estimator to lead our estimating team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing all aspects of the estimating process, ensuring accurate and competitive bids on our construction projects.

Responsibilities:

Lead and manage the estimating team, providing guidance and support as needed.
Oversee the preparation of detailed cost estimates and proposals for construction projects, ensuring accuracy and competitiveness.
Review and analyze project drawings, specifications, and contract documents to identify potential issues, risks, and opportunities for improvement.
Collaborate with clients, architects, engineers, and other project stakeholders to gather project requirements and develop accurate project scopes.
Develop and maintain project budgets, including preparing detailed cost estimates and value engineering options.
Coordinate with subcontractors and suppliers to obtain and evaluate bids and proposals.
Maintain and update company estimating databases, tools, and software.
Continuously monitor industry trends and best practices to ensure the company stays competitive and up-to-date.
Requirements:

Bachelor's degree in Construction Management, Engineering, or a related field.
At least 10 years of experience in the construction industry, with a focus on estimating.
Strong knowledge of construction methods, materials, and pricing.
Proficient in construction estimating and scheduling software, such as On-Screen Takeoff, Bluebeam, or Microsoft Project.
Excellent communication, analytical, and negotiation skills.
Ability to work effectively both independently and as part of a team.
Valid driver's license and willingness to travel as needed.
