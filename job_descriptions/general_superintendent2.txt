Job Title: Construction General Superintendent

Company: YZA General Subcontractor

Description:
YZA General Subcontractor is looking for a knowledgeable and dedicated Construction General Superintendent to join our team. The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing all on-site construction activities, ensuring projects are completed safely, on time, and with exceptional quality.

Responsibilities:

Manage daily on-site construction operations for multiple projects, ensuring work is completed according to project specifications and schedules.
Supervise and coordinate construction crews, subcontractors, and other on-site personnel, providing guidance and direction as needed.
Develop, implement, and monitor project schedules, making adjustments as required to meet changing conditions and deadlines.
Enforce compliance with health and safety regulations, company policies, and industry standards.
Collaborate with project managers, clients, architects, and other project stakeholders to address project-related issues and ensure smooth project execution.
Inspect work in progress to maintain quality control and identify potential issues or areas for improvement.
Keep accurate and up-to-date project documentation, such as daily logs, progress reports, and safety records.
Conduct project closeout procedures, including punch lists, final inspections, and warranty documentation.
Requirements:

High school diploma or equivalent; further education in construction management, engineering, or a related field is preferred.
At least 10 years of experience in the construction industry, including a minimum of 5 years in a supervisory or leadership role.
Comprehensive knowledge of construction processes, materials, and safety regulations.
Ability to read and interpret construction drawings, specifications, and contracts.
Strong leadership, communication, and conflict resolution skills.
Able to work under pressure and adapt to changing priorities and deadlines.
Valid driver's license and willingness to travel to various project sites as needed.
